## Purpose
Efficiently synthesize multi-source information (docs, podcasts) into usable insights.

## Steps
1. Identify sources and questions.
2. Extract key claims and evidence (with attributions).
3. Synthesize themes and gaps.
4. Produce recommendations or actions.
5. Note uncertainties or follow-ups.
