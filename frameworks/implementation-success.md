## Purpose
Guide district implementation of curricular products from kickoff to sustained outcomes.

## Phases
1. Readiness & Stakeholder Alignment
2. Launch Plan (PD, materials, communications)
3. Coaching & Support Cadence
4. Data Review & Instructional Adjustments
5. Sustain & Scale (leadership routines, community engagement)

## Inputs
- District goals, adoption details, timelines, staff capacity, baseline data.

## Artifacts
- Implementation plan, PD calendar, coaching cadence, data review schedule, leadership brief.

## Anti-Patterns
- Overloading staff, unclear ownership, missing feedback loops.
