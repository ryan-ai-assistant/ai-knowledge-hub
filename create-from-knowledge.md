## Purpose
Generate new artifacts by reusing accumulated knowledge.

## Steps
1. Retrieve prior artifacts and notes.
2. Identify reusable modules (tone, structure, how-tos).
3. Draft with explicit references.
4. Review against goals and audience.
5. Package and link related resources.
