## Purpose
Consistently and clearly answer content-related questions from external district partners.

## Steps
1. Clarify the question and context (grade, program, assessment).
2. Reference authoritative guidance (curriculum documentation, policy).
3. Provide a direct answer with rationale.
4. Add examples or short how-to steps.
5. Offer next-step support or resources.
